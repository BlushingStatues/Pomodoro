This tool is based off the famous
time management method The Pomodoro Technique.

Here's how to use it:


Decide on a task to work on.
Start the timer, work for 25 mins, then take a 5 min break.
When you've worked for 4 blocks of 25 mins,
have an extra a long break.
Enjoy.
